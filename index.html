How we make it happen

We are obsessed with extraordinary impact. But we know that issue breakthrough require different environments. Invention has historically prospered in labs isolated from market pressures. Whereas competition and consumers drive innovation.
We do both. Shaking, not stirring.

DÓBRA GrandPrix

In open-source collaboration with hundreds of people around the world we jointly INVENT solutions to the challenging issue. IdeaChain tools helps everyone share their own vision, work together and get rewarded in tokens. For those who's ready to go further, we have Dobra Studio

DÓBRA Studio

We form full-stacked teams around a dozen of awesome concept solutions to GrandPrix's issue, prototype viable first product, run market test and scale the most impactful ones together with VC and strategic partners. All teams get guidance, support, funds and equity stake to INNOVATE.

DÓBRA PERKS

THE WORLD COMES TOGETHER

Thousands of people share thoughts and co-invent impactful solutions to the issues. These are not only entrepreneurially inclined people, but don't underestimate them.

EXTRAORDINARY SOLUTIONS FIRST

We engage cross-domain experts, add AI and allow creative teams to have a blast. As they all deal with the same issue it creates the intrinsic magic of startup survival.

FOCUSING ON STRENGTHS

DÓBRA team works as a part of the startup teams, shutting down all operational and funding tasks. The combination of AI and human touch reinforces solutions on the go.

EVERYONE CAN GET REWARDED

Grand Prix participants can receive cash rewards whether or not they want to be involved in future startups. Up to 40% equity stake are secured for startup teams.

PROPERLY COOKED STARTUPS

Legal and financial matters, including IP, transparent traction reports and other aspects necessary for a partnership or investment deals are always in order.

deep partnership everywhere

Pro-bono, IP-sharing and co-funding partnerships permeate every stage, from providing testbeds for prototyping to reaching the first customers.
